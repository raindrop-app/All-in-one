Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

Information We Collect

Rain Drop collects minimal data necessary to provide our digital cartography and knowledge mapping services. We gather information you provide when creating an account, including your name and email address.

When you use our platform to map thought architecture and organize information flow, we store your content, maps, and organizational structures. This data remains private and is used solely to deliver our services to you.

How We Use Your Information

Your data enables us to provide, maintain, and improve Rain Drop's knowledge mapping capabilities. We use your information to authenticate your account, sync your thought maps across devices, and personalize your experience.

We analyze aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to enhance our digital cartography tools and develop new features that better serve our community of knowledge architects.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Rain Drop uses essential cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences. These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly and cannot be disabled.

We also employ analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our knowledge mapping interface. You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Analytics

We use privacy-focused analytics services to understand user behavior and improve our digital cartography platform. These services collect anonymized data about page views, feature usage, and navigation patterns.

All analytics data is aggregated and stripped of personally identifiable information before analysis. We do not share individual user data with third-party analytics providers.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your thought maps and personal information. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS protocols and at rest using AES-256 encryption.

Our infrastructure undergoes regular security audits, and we maintain strict access controls to ensure only authorized personnel can access systems containing user data.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user of Rain Drop, you have comprehensive rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access your personal information and knowledge maps at any time
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Export your thought architecture and maps in portable formats
  • Object to processing of your personal information
  • Restrict how we process your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account information and knowledge maps for as long as your account remains active. If you choose to delete your account, all personal data and content will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Backup copies are maintained for disaster recovery purposes and are automatically deleted after 90 days. You can request immediate data deletion by contacting our privacy team.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated via email and prominently displayed on our platform.

Continued use of Rain Drop after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
